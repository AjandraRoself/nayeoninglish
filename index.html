👑 Who is Nayeon?

  • Full Name: Im Na-yeon

📅 Career Timeline

2015 - TWICE Debut

Selected through "SIXTEEN" reality show and debuted with TWICE

2015-2022 - Group Success

June 2022 - Solo Pioneer

First TWICE member to release solo album "IM NAYEON"

June 2024 - Continued Success
